Get to us by car

From the city of Bergen, head north following the E39 route

When you get to Isdalstø, come off the E39 and join the 565

follow this route until the 565 splits off into 568 then follow that until you see the signs for 'Kattegat - Viking Experience'

Using Public Transport

If you are in any of the surrounding towns/ villages there is plenty of public transport options to reach us, there is also a local shuttle service from the nearby resort Øksnes friluftsområde
